About this page

Each surface observation is paired with the AROME Arctic 2.5 km deterministic forecast (00 Z and 12 Z runs). The nearest model grid point is used (max 0.5° distance accepted). RMSE = root-mean-square error, MAE = mean absolute error, BIAS = mean(model − obs) — positive bias means the model is too high. Statistics are updated once daily at 06:00 UTC.
